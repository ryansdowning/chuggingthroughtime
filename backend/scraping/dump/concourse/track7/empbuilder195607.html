The Milwaukee Road had introduced full-length "Super Domes" on its Olympian Hiawatha as early as 1953, and in 1954 the Northern Pacific's North Coast Limited was re-equipped with Vista-Dome coaches and sleepers. Great Northern rose to the competitive challenge with its "Great Domes", introduced in the summer and fall of 1955.

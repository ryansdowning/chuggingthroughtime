NOTE: Connecting train from Williams Junction to Grand Canyon makes last trip on September 24 [1966]. Twice daily motor bus service available year round.
The California Special was one of the trains Santa Fe targeted for elimination after the cancellation of Post Office mail contracts in September 1967. First the through cars were eliminated; passengers had to physically transfer to and from the San Francisco Chief in Clovis. Santa Fe then petitioned to drop the train entirely, and service ended in July of 1968.

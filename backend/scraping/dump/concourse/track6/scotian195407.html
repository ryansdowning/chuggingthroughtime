THROUGH CAR SERVICE
Coaches are Operated on All Trains, except "The Ocean Limited."
Regularly assigned cars on all trains are Air-Conditioned unless indicated N A C (Non Air-Conditioned).

Nos. 59 and 60 - "THE SCOTIAN"—Halifax and Montreal.

Sleeping Cars...Halifax and Montreal—[Nos. 5902 and 6002] 10-Sections, 1 Drawing-room, 1 Compartment.

Campbellton and Levis—[Nos. 5900 and 6000] 8-Sections, 4 Double Bedrooms. (Open at Levis at 9:30 p.m., and until 7:00 a.m.)

Charlottetown to Montreal—[No. 3901] 10-Sections, 1 Drawing-room, 1 Compartment. (Except Sundays from Charlottetown. In No. 39 to Moncton.)

Boston and Halifax—("The Gull") (Nos. 5904 and 234) 10-Sections, 1 Drawing-room, 2 Compartments. (Except Saturdays from Boston. In 14 to Moncton. Except Sundays from Halifax. In 13 from Moncton.)

Dining Car...Halifax and Montreal.

Coach Lunch Service available between Halifax and Montreal.

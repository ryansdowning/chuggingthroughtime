Note: Times shown on New York Central timetable pages are Eastern Daylight Savings Time. Times shown on T.H. & B. and Canadian Pacific timetable pages are Eastern Standard Time. However, do be advised that Toronto, Hamilton, Welland, and Fort Erie observe Daylight Savings Time between April 25 and October 31 [1965], as does Black Rock, NY. Local time in those towns will be one hour later than times shown in this timetable while Daylight Savings Time is in effect.

Note: There is a 13.7 mile discrepancy between the New York Central and Canadian Pacific timetables in the mileage from Buffalo to Welland and Toronto. Figures used in this timetable are NYC mileage to Welland, TH & B mileage from Welland to Hamilton, and Canadian Pacific mileage from Hamilton to Toronto.

LEGEND

Time:

A - A.M.

D - Train stops only to discharge passengers

F - Conditional (Flag) stop

I  - Customs/Immigration inspection stop

M - Meal stop

N - Not a passenger stop

P - P.M.

R - Train stops only to receive passengers


Services:

A - Auto loading/unloading facilities (as freight)

B - Checked baggage is handled

C - Coupon station (Ticket office)

I  - International border inspection station

M - Food service available

P - Telephone station

R - Rental cars available

T - Telegraph station

X - Checked baggage NOT handled

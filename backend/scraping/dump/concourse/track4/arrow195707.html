This timetable is related to the Union Pacific 1957 Summer Tours series, but only peripherally so...I was beginning to work on a timetable for trains 7-5 and 6-8, the remnants of the Los Angeles Limited, and realized that to be complete I ought to include the connecting service to Omaha operated by the Milwaukee Road. Since this timetable was simpler it was finished first; the connecting trains will be coming Real Soon Now are now up and ready on Track 8.

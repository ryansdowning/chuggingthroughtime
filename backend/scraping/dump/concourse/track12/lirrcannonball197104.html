It also helped that, starting from the front of the book, this is the first passenger service listing one comes to!

Train No. 16 is designated in the timetable as "The Cannonball"...and if it operated according to this schedule, it certainly was. At least between East Hampton and Amagansett...3.3 miles in just two minutes, or 99 MPH average including station dwell time! Considering how leisurely the remainder of the schedule was, though, I think it likely that this 99 MPH stretch was a misprint or a clerical error.

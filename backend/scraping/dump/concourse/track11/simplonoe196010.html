NOTE: At Paris, only takes passengers for stations beyond Milan (in 2nd class, only for Venice and beyond) and vice versa.

NOTE for No. 6 at Vallorbe: Arrival time in original is a misprint (5 50); time shown is my best guess from comparisons with other trains.

NOTE: Mileage at Plati is a misprint in the original table 897 (shows as 12 miles from Gevgeli); mileage shown here is computed from table 895.

NOTE: Connecting train 2404 at Postojna is a Railcar.

NOTE: The UK observes Summer Time (Daylight Savings Time), times at UK stations will be one hour later from April 16.

NOTE for train 4: "SIMPLON-ORIENT EXPRESS. (On Mondays, Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays, passengers change at Thessaloniki.)"

NOTE for train 3: "SIMPLON-ORIENT EXPRESS. Runs Mondays, Thursdays and Saturdays, Athens-Paris (on other days, change at Thessaloniki)."

NOTE: Be advised of some minor (less than ten miles maximum) inconsistencies in mileages between several timetables.

DAYS OF OPERATION:

Daily:

1st class sleeping car and 1st class carriage Paris-Rome and vice versa.

1st class sleeping car Paris-Naples and vice versa.

1st class sleeping car and 2nd class Couchette car Paris-Venice and vice versa.

1st and 2nd class carriages and 2nd class Couchette car Paris-Trieste and vice versa.

1st and 2nd class carriages Belgrade-Thessaloniki and vice versa.

1st and 2nd class carriages Venice-Belgrade and vice versa.

1st and 2nd class carriages Belgrade-Sofia and vice versa.

1st and 2nd class carriages Rijeka-Trieste

Restaurant Car Paris-Dôle and vice versa.

Restaurant Car Venice-Milan and Rome-Bologna.

Light refreshments available Verona-Trieste and Bologna-Rome.

Restaurant Car Rome-Naples and Caserta-(dep. 12:40 p.m.)-Rome.

Restaurant Car (Yugoslav) Sežana-Belgrade and vice versa.

Restaurant Car Thessaloniki-Larissa and vice versa.

Saturdays only: 1st and 2nd class carriages Paris-Thessaloniki.

Tuesdays and Saturdays: 1st class sleeping car Paris-Belgrade.

Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays: 1st class sleeping car and 1st and 2nd class carriages Paris-Athens.

Tuesdays, Thursdays and Sundays: 1st and 2nd class carriages Paris-Istanbul.

Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays: 1st and 2nd class carriages Belgrade-Istanbul.

Thursdays and Sundays: 1st class sleeping car Paris-Istanbul.

Thursdays only: 1st and 2nd class carriages Thessaloniki-Paris.

Tuesdays and Fridays: 1st class sleeping car Belgrade-Paris.

Mondays, Thursdays and Saturdays: 1st class sleeping car and 1st and 2nd class carriages Athens-Paris.

Tuesdays, Fridays and Sundays: 1st and 2nd class carriages Istanbul-Paris.

Tuesdays and Fridays: 1st class sleeping car Istanbul-Paris.

Tuesdays, Fridays and Sundays: 1st and 2nd class carriages Istanbul-Belgrade.


In Turkey: Operates from Sofia on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays; operates from Istanbul on Tuesdays, Fridays and Sundays. Operates daily Istanbul-Edirne and vice versa.

The Simplon-Orient Express as shown here is in the twilight of its career. In 1962 it would be withdrawn and a slower train service, named the "Direct Orient Express", would take its place.

LEGEND

Time:

A - A.M.

D - Train stops only to discharge passengers

F - Conditional (Flag) stop

I  - Customs/Immigration inspection stop

M - Meal stop

N - Not a passenger stop

P - P.M.

R - Train stops only to receive passengers


Services:

A - Auto loading/unloading facilities (as freight)

B - Checked baggage is handled

C - Coupon station (Ticket office)

I  - International border inspection station

M - Food service available

P - Telephone station

R - Rental cars available

T - Telegraph station

X - Checked baggage NOT handled

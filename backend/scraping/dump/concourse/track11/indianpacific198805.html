Note: Mileages in this timetable are computed from kilometers shown in the original and have been rounded to the nearest mile.

Standards of time:
AEST: Australian Eastern Standard Time (UTC+10)
ACST: Australian Central Standard Time (UTC+9:30)
WST: Western Standard Time (UTC+8)

Daylight savings time in effect as follows:

New South Wales: +1 hour from Oct. 25, 1987 to March 20, 1988 and from Oct. 30, 1988 to March 19, 1989.

South Australia: +1 hour from Oct. 25, 1987 to March 20, 1988 and from Oct. 30, 1988 to March 19, 1989.

Not in effect in Western Australia during this time period.

INDIAN PACIFIC
Australian National Railways, State Rail Authority of New South Wales,
Western Australian Government Railways (Westrail).

Runs on Thursdays, Saturdays and Sundays from Sydney,
Mondays, Thursdays and Sundays from Perth.

Westbound:

Departing Sydney Thursdays:

Sleeping Cars—First class only, Sydney-Perth.

Lounge—First class only, Sydney-Perth.

Restaurant Car—Sydney-Perth.

Through Carriages (First class and Economy class)—Sydney-Adelaide.

Through Carriage (Economy class)—Adelaide-Perth. (Departs Adelaide Friday afternoon.)

Automobile Carriers—Adelaide-Perth. (Departs Adelaide Friday afternoon.)


Departing Sydney Saturdays:

Sleeping Cars—First class only, Sydney-Perth.

Lounge—First class only, Sydney-Perth.

Restaurant Car—Sydney-Perth.

Through Carriage (Economy class)—Broken Hill-Adelaide (Departs Broken Hill Sunday morning.)

Automobile Carriers—Adelaide-Perth. (Departs Adelaide Sunday afternoon.)


Departing Sydney Sundays:

Sleeping Cars—First class and Economy class, Sydney-Perth.

Lounge—First class and Economy class, Sydney-Perth.

Restaurant Car—Sydney-Perth.

Through Carriages (First class and Economy class)—Sydney-Adelaide.

Through Carriage (Economy class)—Adelaide-Perth. (Departs Adelaide Monday afternoon.)

Automobile Carriers—Adelaide-Perth. (Departs Adelaide Monday afternoon.)


Eastbound:

Departing Perth Mondays:

Sleeping Cars—First class and Economy class, Perth-Sydney.

Lounge—First class and Economy class, Perth-Sydney.

Restaurant Car—Perth-Sydney.

Through Carriage (Economy class)—Perth-Adelaide.

Through Carriage (Economy class)—Adelaide-Broken Hill. (Departs Adelaide Wednesday noon.)

Automobile Carriers—Perth-Adelaide.


Departing Perth Thursdays:

Sleeping Cars—First class only, Perth-Sydney.

Lounge—First class only, Perth-Sydney.

Restaurant Car—Perth-Sydney.

Through Carriages (First class and Economy class)—Adelaide-Sydney. (Departs Adelaide Saturday noon.)

Automobile Carriers—Perth-Adelaide.


Departing Perth Sundays:

Sleeping Cars—First class only, Perth-Sydney.

Lounge—First class only, Perth-Sydney.

Restaurant Car—Perth-Sydney

Through Carriage (Economy Class)—Perth-Adelaide.

Through Carriages (First class and Economy Class)—Adelaide-Sydney. (Departs Adelaide Tuesday noon.)

Automobile Carriers—Perth-Adelaide.


TRANS AUSTRALIAN
Australian National Railways & Western Australian Government Railways (Westrail)

NOTE: The Trans Australian operates on the same schedule as the Indian Pacific, but on differing days of the week.

Runs on Wednesdays and Saturdays from both Adelaide and Perth.

Sleeping Cars—First class and Economy, Adelaide-Perth and v.v.
Lounge—First class and Economy, Adelaide-Perth and v.v.
Through Carriages—Economy class, Adelaide-Perth and v.v.
Restaurant Car—Adelaide-Perth and v.v.
Automobile Carriers—Adelaide-Perth and v.v.

LEGEND

Time:

A - A.M.

D - Train stops only to discharge passengers

F - Conditional (Flag) stop

I  - Customs/Immigration inspection stop

M - Meal stop

N - Not a passenger stop

P - P.M.

R - Train stops only to receive passengers


Services:

A - Auto loading/unloading facilities (as freight)

B - Checked baggage is handled

C - Coupon station (Ticket office)

I  - International border inspection station

M - Food service available

P - Telephone station

R - Rental cars available

T - Telegraph station

X - Checked baggage NOT handled

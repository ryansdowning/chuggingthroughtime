LEGEND

Time:

A - A.M.

D - Train stops only to discharge passengers

F - Conditional (Flag) stop

N - Not a passenger stop

P - P.M.

R - Train stops only to receive passengers


Services:

A - Auto loading/unloading facilities (as freight)

B - Checked baggage is handled

C - Coupon station (Ticket office)

M - Meal stop

P - Telephone station

R - Rental cars available

T - Telegraph station

X - Checked baggage NOT handled






This was the running mate of the East Coast Champion which served Tampa and St. Petersburg on the Gulf Coast of Florida. As mentioned on the other train's page, the term Tamiami refers to the heavyweight coach-and-Pullman train which the Champion supplanted on the A.C.L.'s time card.

Time:

A - A.M.

D - Train stops only to discharge passengers

F - Conditional (Flag) stop

N - Not a passenger stop

P - P.M.

R - Train stops only to receive passengers


Services:

A - Auto loading/unloading facilities (as freight)

B - Checked baggage is handled

C - Coupon station (Ticket office)

M - Meal stop

P - Telephone station

R - Rental cars available

T - Telegraph station

X - Checked baggage NOT handled





This is the final timetable for the Silver Star as a privately owned and operated train; however, it would be one of several Florida streamliners picked up and continued by Amtrak. It continues in operation today under the same name, albeit under a different intermediate routing.

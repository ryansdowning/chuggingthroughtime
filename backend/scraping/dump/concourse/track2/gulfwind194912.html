The original schedule of the Gulf Wind was timed to permit connections with Seaboard's Silver Meteor at Jacksonville. Not shown are connections at New Orleans with the Southern Pacific and Missouri Pacific/Santa Fe providing service to Texas, the Grand Canyon, and California.

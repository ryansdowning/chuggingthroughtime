This is the last edition of the California Zephyr in my collection. The train would complete its final run on March 22, 1970. With its discontinuance, Western Pacific shed its passenger service entirely and became a freight-only railroad while Burlington combined what remained of its California service with a secondary Chicago-Omaha train. The Denver and Rio Grande portion, however, would continue well into the Amtrak days as the tri-weekly Rio Grande Zephyr.

CALIFORNIA ZEPHYR CHARGE

The following California Zephyr Charges will be assessed:
$15.00 First-Class, $5.00 Coach - Between stations on the CB&Q and stations on the WP.

Note:  All space on the California Zephyr is reserved and specifically assigned in advance.  Coach seat reservations westbound from Chicago must be claimed at train gate by 2 50 p.m. Checked baggage is carried only for through movement between Chicago and Denver or beyond and not to or from intermediate points, except baggage can be checked to or from all regular stops between Omaha and McCook.  No form of free or reduced rate transportation is honored on the California Zephyr June 15-September 15 or December 15-January 15.

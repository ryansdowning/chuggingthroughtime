By the mid-1950s the slowdown in traffic was being felt systemwide. The Washington-Chicago run could no longer support a dedicated premium passenger train. The Liberty Limited name would live on for a few more years, but by the summer of 1956 it would be combined with the New York-Detroit Red Arrow westbound and with the Pennsylvania Limited eastbound.

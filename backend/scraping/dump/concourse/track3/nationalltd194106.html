Time:

A - A.M.

D - Train stops only to discharge passengers

F - Conditional (Flag) stop

M - Meal stop

N - Not a passenger stop

P - P.M.

R - Train stops only to receive passengers


Services:

A - Auto loading/unloading facilities (as freight)

B - Checked baggage is handled

C - Coupon station (Ticket office)

M - Food service available

P - Telephone station

R - Rental cars available

T - Telegraph station

X - Checked baggage NOT handled






The National Limited, along with the Capitol Limited and the Royal Blue, was one of three streamliners introduced by the Baltimore & Ohio in the late 1930s featuring Diesel power and rebuilt heavyweight cars modernized under the direction of industrial designer Otto Kuhler.

Note: The Southern Crescent operates daily between New York and Birmingham and tri-weekly between Birmingham and New Orleans, departing Birmingham for New Orleans on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Sundays and departing New Orleans northbound on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays.

Note: The Amtrak connecting train (Sunset Limited) operates tri-weekly departing New Orleans westbound on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays and arriving Los Angeles on Wednesdays, Fridays and Sundays. The eastbound Sunset Limited departs Los Angeles on Tuesdays, Fridays and Sundays and arrives New Orleans on Thursdays, Sundays and Tuesdays.

NOTE for No. 1 at Sweet Briar: "Stops to discharge revenue passengers from Charlottesville and beyond, on Sundays only."

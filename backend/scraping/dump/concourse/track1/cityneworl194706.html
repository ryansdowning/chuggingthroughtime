The streamlined all-coach City of New Orleans made its debut in April of 1947. With a hot 16 hour schedule between Chicago and New Orleans which trimmed nearly 9 full hours off the running time of its predecessor the Creole, it was even faster than the flagship Panama Limited of similar vintage.

From the pages of the Official Guide, June 1947
